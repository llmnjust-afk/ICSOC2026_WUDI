\caption{Slack-safety region (mock data, Theorem~\ref{thm:slack}).

$\sigma_{\min}$ reaches $H+\tau_{\max}$ (dotted line); below that
boundary the controller defers jobs whose slack is too tight and
violates. Carbon (M1, solid) degrades monotonically as $\sigma_{\min}$
rises above the safety boundary, because more jobs are admitted
immediately rather than deferred. The operator sets $\sigma_{\min}$ at
the boundary to get SLA safety at the carbon price of plateau-region
savings; lowering $\sigma_{\min}$ into the violation region trades SLA
risk for additional carbon reduction.}
